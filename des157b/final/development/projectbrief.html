Project Brief

Project Summary

I intend on making a desktop interface that poses several questions to the user about their mental health and what can help them.

Project Goals

The goal for my project is to be a source of comfort for the user when they feel very anxious or when they are at a low point mentally.

Design Strategies

I want the interface to spark joy with the user, and to do that I want to incorporate the use of more vibrant colors such as blues, oranges, or pinks. I am hoping these colors will produce more contrast against the content of the interface. I also want to incorporate more san-serif typefaces in order to achieve proper legibility against more colorful backgrounds.

Interaction Strategies

The user will have multiple questions posed to them about their mental health when opening the interface. After these questions the user will choose a coping mechanism from the options provided in order to help with their mental health. I plan on making the choices about 10 to 15 minutes long, in order for the user to have a set time of relaxation.

Experience Goals

My goal is for those dealing with their mental health to have a moment of calmness when they interact with this interface. I plan on achieving that by providing multiple experiences that could help the user to take a timed break or find a moment for mindfulness.
